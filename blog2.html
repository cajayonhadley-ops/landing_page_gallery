Playing With Heart: My Journey as a Student-Athlete

Introduction

Basketball is more than just a game for many Filipinos, including me. I grew up dreaming of playing not just to win, but to prove that I am capable. For me, basketball became a passion that shaped who I am today. This blog shares my personal experiences and challenges as a student-athlete, especially when passion meets expectations, height standards, and the responsibilities of school life.

Passion for Basketball

My love for basketball started at a young age. Kahit simpleng court lang sa barangay, sapat na iyon para mangarap ako. Naalala ko noong gumagawa ako ng sarili kong court at isinasabit sa kung saan-saan para lang makapagbasketball. Basketball became my escape from stress, lalo na kapag pagod ako sa school. It also helped me meet new friends and express myself. What keeps me playing is not fame or money, but the passion I feel every time I step on the court. My family motivates me to pursue my dream in basketball. Like many Filipino athletes, I continue to play because:

  • Basketball gives me a sense of purpose.
  • It teaches discipline and teamwork.
  • It keeps me motivated despite challenges.

The Reality of Being a Student-Athlete

Hindi madali para sa akin ang maging student-athlete. Kailangan kong pagsabayin ang academics at training, kaya madalas ramdam ko ang pagod sa katawan at isip. May mga araw na halos wala nang lakas pagkatapos ng practice, pero kailangan ko pa ring mag-aral para sa exams at tapusin ang school requirements. Time management ang isa sa pinakamalaking challenge ko. As a student-athlete, here are some of my struggles and experiences:

  • Balancing academics and training every single day.
  • Feeling physically and mentally exhausted after practice.
  • Time management is one of my biggest challenges.
The Height Problem and Silent Disappointment

Dumating ang panahon ng COVID kung saan ilang buwan akong nawalan ng basketball dahil sa lockdown. Matapos ang lockdown, nagkaroon ulit ako ng tryout sa aming school para sa senior high school division, pero hindi ako pinalad. Sinabihan ako na wala na raw spot para sa posisyon ko dahil karamihan sa mga nakuha ay mas matatangkad.

Dahil sa nangyari, naapektuhan ang confidence ko. Nagkaroon ako ng self-doubt at pakiramdam ko ay limitado ang opportunities ko. Masakit tanggapin na kahit ibigay ko ang lahat ng effort, parang hindi pa rin sapat dahil sa standards na wala akong kontrol. Dito ko naranasan ang matinding disappointment at napatanong ako sa sarili ko kung dapat ko pa bang ipagpatuloy ang pangarap ko sa basketball.

  • Height became a major factor that limited my chances despite my skills and effort.
  • The rejection affected my confidence and made me question my worth as a player.
  • I experienced silent disappointment but learned how painful standards beyond my control can be.
Conclusion / Reflection

Ang struggles ng Filipino athletes bago ang success ay madalas hindi nakikita ng iba. Passion lang minsan ang pinanghahawakan namin para magpatuloy, at kahit maraming hadlang, ito ang nagbibigay sa amin ng dahilan para gumising araw-araw at magsikap. Natutunan ko na kahit may limitasyon sa tangkad, resources, at opportunities, ang tunay na sukatan ng isang atleta ay ang dedication, perseverance, at hindi pagsuko.

Kahit mabagal ang progress at maraming setbacks, alam kong bawat hirap, bawat pagkatalo, at bawat sakripisyo ay bahagi ng paghubog sa kung sino ako ngayon at sino ang gusto kong maging sa hinaharap. Natutunan ko rin na mahalaga ang suporta ng pamilya at kaibigan; sila ang nagbibigay lakas at inspirasyon sa akin.
